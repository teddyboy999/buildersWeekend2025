# About Us

Welcome to our company! We are a team of passionate designers, developers, and data scientists dedicated to creating innovative solutions for inventory management.

## Our Mission
Our mission is to revolutionize inventory management by leveraging AI to predict stock requirements, optimize restocking schedules, and reduce waste.


## Our SKU Management Service
We are developing a cutting-edge **Stock Keeping Unit (SKU) Management Service** that uses AI to predict when you need to restock items. 
Simply input the product ID, expiration date, and the number of days you need to maintain inventory, and our system will handle the rest.
